Different Categories of Cycling
Mountain Biking
My favorite category by far. Involves trails, the great outdoors, suspension and a little adrenaline.
Road Cycling
Very fast. What most people think about when they think of cycling. A bunch of poeple with light bikes, tight clothing and strong shaved legs.
Commuting
For many, bikes are means of transportation. Getting from point a to point b gives you a small workout and a chance to enjoy the city streets.
